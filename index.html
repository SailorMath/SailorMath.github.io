The SailorMath Blog

Welcome to The SailorMath Blog!

As a math enthusiast and sailor, I’m excited to share my passion for both of these worlds with you. Through this blog, I’ll be exploring the intersection of math and sailing, discussing interesting problems and solutions, and sharing my insights and experiences.

Whether you’re a sailor who’s interested in the math behind the sport, a math enthusiast who’s curious about the applications of math in sailing, or just someone who loves learning new things, this blog is for you.

Join me on this voyage of discovery as we explore the fascinating world of math and sailing together. Fair winds and following seas!
